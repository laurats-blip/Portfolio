About Me

Hi! I’m Laura Torres, a graphic designer in progress and a visual world explorer. I’m currently 19 years old and I love bringing characters to life in 3D, playing with bold colors, and designing packaging that tells stories.

My creativity is powered by music — from rock to pop — depending on the mood, animals, and random ideas that pop into my head at any moment. I believe design should make you feel something, and that’s why I’m always experimenting, learning, and letting new projects surprise me.

This portfolio is the place where I share what I imagine... and everything I’m still excited to create.
